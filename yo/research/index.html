Iwadi Irin-ajo & Awọn anfani Ilera

Ẹri imọ-jinlẹ ti n ṣe atilẹyin irin-ajo bi ọkan ninu awọn ilowosi ilera ti o lagbara julọ ti o wa

Irin-ajo kii ṣe adaṣe nikan - o jẹ idasi ilera ti imọ-jinlẹ pẹlu awọn ipa nla lori igbesi aye gigun, ilera inu ọkan ati ẹjẹ, iṣẹ oye, ati ilera ti iṣelọpọ. Oju-iwe yii ṣajọpọ iwadii gige-eti lori awọn atupale irin-ajo, gait biomechanics, ati awọn abajade ilera.

Cadence ati Kikankikan: Awọn Igbesẹ 100/Ibagbede Iṣẹju

Ipinnu CADENCE-Awọn agbalagba

Ọkan ninu awọn iwadii aipẹ ti o ṣe pataki julọ ni imọ-jinlẹ irin-ajo ni idanimọ tiAwọn igbesẹ 100 fun iṣẹju kan (spm) gẹgẹbi iloro gbogbo agbaye fun irin-ajo iwọntunwọnsi.

Wiwa bọtini: 100 spm = Imudara Iwọntunwọnsi

Iwadi CADENCE-Awọn agbalagba (Tudor-Locke et al., 2019) ṣe idanwo awọn agbalagba 76 ti ọjọ-ori 21-40 o si rii pe:

  • 100 igbesẹ / minni ibamu si3 MET(kikanra dede)
  • 86% ifamọ ati 89,6% patofun idamo dede-kikankikan aṣayan iṣẹ-ṣiṣe
  • Ipele-ilẹ yii jẹ deede ni iyalẹnu laarin awọn ọjọ-ori 21-85 ọdun
  • 130 igbese / minni ibamu si6 MET(kikankikan to lagbara)

Ipeye diẹ sii Ju Awọn iṣiro Ipilẹṣẹ Iyara

Moore et al. (2021) ṣe agbekalẹ idogba ti iṣelọpọ ti cadence ti o jẹ23-35% diẹ sii deedeju idogba orisun iyara ACSM ti aṣa:

METs = 0.0219 × cadence (igbesẹ / iṣẹju) + 0,72

Ipeye: ± 0.5 METs ni awọn iyara irin-ajo deede

Idogba yii n ṣiṣẹ nitori cadence taara ṣe afihan igbohunsafẹfẹ gbigbe ati inawo agbara, lakoko ti iyara le ni ipa nipasẹ iyipada gigun gigun ati ilẹ.

Iyara Gait: “Ami pataki” ti Ilera

Iyara Irinse Awọn asọtẹlẹ Iwalaaye

Ni ọdun 2011, iwadi JAMA ala-ilẹ ti34.485 agbalagba agbalagbaIyara gait ti iṣeto bi asọtẹlẹ ti o lagbara ti iku, ti n gba ni yiyan ti a"Ami pataki" ti ilera(Studenski et al., 2011).

Lominu ni Ala

  • <0.8 m/s (1.8 mph): Ga niyen ewu, arinbo idiwọn
  • 0.8-1.0 m/s (1.8-2.2 mph): Ewu dede, awọn idiwọn iṣẹ
  • 1.0-1.3 m/s (2.2-2.9 mph): Ti o dara iṣẹ-ilera
  • > 1.3 m/s (2.9 mph)Atọka ilera ti o dara julọ, eewu iku kekere

Iyara-Iwalaaye Gradient

Fun gbogbo 0.1 m/s ilosoke ninu gait iyara, nibẹ ni isunmọ a12% idinku ninu eewu iku. Ibasepo yii jẹ laini laini laini ati dimu kọja awọn olugbe oriṣiriṣi, ṣiṣe iyara gait ọkan ninu awọn asọtẹlẹ ẹyọkan ti o lagbara julọ ti igbesi aye gigun.

Awọn Iyipada Ọdọọdun Sọ asọtẹlẹ Irẹdanu Isubu

Iwadi aipẹ (Verghese et al., 2023) fihan iyẹnlododun sile ni mọnran iyarapaapaa jẹ asọtẹlẹ awọn isubu ju iyara pipe lọ. Idinku ti> 0.05 m/s fun ọdun kan awọn ifihan agbara pọ si eewu isubu ni pataki, ti o mu ki ilowosi tete ṣiṣẹ.

Awọn ohun elo isẹgun

Iyara Gait ni bayi ni igbagbogbo ni iwọn ni:

  • Geriatric igbelewọn: Idanimọ ailera ati idinku iṣẹ
  • Titele isodi: Iwọn ipinnu ti ilọsiwaju imularada
  • Ilera inu ọkan ati ẹjẹ: Afihan ti iṣẹ ọkan ọkan ati ipamọ
  • Ayẹwo ewu abẹAwọn iyara <0.8 m/s tọka si eewu iṣẹ abẹ ti o ga julọ

Awọn itọkasi bọtini:

  • Studenski S, et al. (2011).JAMA 305(1):50-58 34.485 olukopa
  • Verghese J, et al. (2023).BMC Geriatr 23:290
  • Pamoukdjian F, et al. (2022).BMC Geriatr 22:394 agboorun awotẹlẹ

Awọn Igbesẹ Ojoojumọ ati Awọn abajade Ilera

Awọn Igbesẹ melo ni O Nilo Lootọ?

Lakoko ti “awọn igbesẹ 10,000” ti di ibi-afẹde aṣa, iwadii aipẹ ṣe afihan aworan nuanced diẹ sii:

Awọn Ifojusi Igbesẹ Ti O Da Ẹri

  • 4.400 igbesẹ / ọjọAwọn anfani iku bẹrẹ (Lee et al., 2019)
  • 7,000-8,000 igbesẹ / ọjọIwọn to dara julọ fun ọpọlọpọ awọn agbalagba (Inoue et al., 2023)
  • 8,000-9,000 igbesẹ / ọjọ: Plateau ojuami - afikun awọn igbesẹ ti pese diminishing padà
  • 10,000+ igbesẹ / ọjọ: Ko si afikun anfani iku ni awọn agbalagba agbalagba

Peak-30 Cadence: Metiriki Dara ju Awọn Igbesẹ Lapapọ lọ?

Iwadi ilẹ-ilẹ nipasẹ Del Pozo-Cruz et al. (2022) itupalẹ78.500 UK agbalagbaṣe afihan metiriki tuntun kan:Oke-30 cadence- iwọn apapọ lakoko akoko irin-ajo iṣẹju 30 ti o dara julọ lojoojumọ.

Peak-30 Cadence awari

Peak-30 cadence wàominira ni nkanpẹlu idinku ailera ati iku, paapaa lẹhin iṣakoso fun awọn igbesẹ lapapọ. Awọn iloro bọtini:

  • 80 spm Oke-30: Ipilẹṣẹ
  • 100 sp Peak-30: 30% idinku ninu eewu iku
  • 120+ spm Peak-30Anfani to pọju (40% idinku)

Itumọ:Idaduro iwọntunwọnsi-si-agbara irinse (paapaa o kan iṣẹju 30 fun ọjọ kan) le ṣe pataki ju awọn igbesẹ ojoojumọ lọ lapapọ.

Igbesẹ ati Idena Arun Onibaje

Titunto si et al. (2022) ṣe atupale data Eto Iwadi Gbogbo Wa ti n ṣafihan awọn iṣiro igbesẹ idaduro dinku eewu ti:

  • Àtọgbẹ: 40-50% idinku ewu pẹlu 8,000+ awọn igbesẹ / ọjọ
  • Isanraju: 35% ewu idinku
  • apnea orun: 30% ewu idinku
  • GERD: 25% ewu idinku
  • Ibanujẹ: 20% ewu idinku

Igbesẹ ati Ilera Ọpọlọ

Del Pozo-Cruz et al. (2022) rii pe awọn igbesẹ ojoojumọ mejeeji ATI kikankikan igbesẹ (cadence) ni nkan ṣe pẹludinku ewu iyawere:

  • 9.800 igbesẹ / ọjọIwọn to dara julọ fun idena iyawere (idinku eewu 50%)
  • Ti o ga agbara: Afikun 20-30% idinku eewu kọja kika kika nikan
  • Iwọn to munadoko ti o kere julọ: Awọn igbesẹ 3,800 / ọjọ ti irin-ajo brisk (≥100 spm)

Ikẹkọ Irin-ajo aarin (IWT)

Dara ju Irinse Tesiwaju?

Ikẹkọ Irin-ajo Aarin (IWT) awọn akoko miiran ti irin-ajo iyara (≥70% VO₂max tabi ~ 120+ spm) pẹlu irin-ajo lọra (~ 80 spm), ni igbagbogbo ni awọn aaye arin iṣẹju 3. Iwadi nipasẹ Karstoft et al. (2024) fihan IWT pesesuperior ilera anfaniakawe si lemọlemọfún dede-kikankikan irinse.

Awọn anfani IWT vs Irinse Ilọsiwaju

AbajadeIWT IlọsiwajuTesiwaju Irinse
VO₂max ilosoke+15-20%+8-10%
Agbara iṣan+12%+5%
Idinku HbA1c (T2D)-0.8%-0.3%
Pipadanu sanra ara-2,5 kg-1.0 kg

Niyanju IWT Ilana

IWT ipilẹ:

  • Dara ya:5 min irin-ajo ti o rọrun
  • Awọn aaye arin:Omiiran iṣẹju 3 yiyara (≥120 spm) + 3 min o lọra (80 spm) × 5 ṣeto
  • Fara bale:5 min irin-ajo ti o rọrun
  • Igbohunsafẹfẹ:4-5 ọjọ / ọsẹ
  • Lapapọ akoko:40 iṣẹju fun igba

IWT ti ilọsiwaju:

  • Awọn aaye arin:2 min iyara pupọ (130+ spm) + Iwọntunwọnsi iṣẹju 2 (100 spm) × 8 ṣeto
  • Ilọsiwaju:Ṣe alekun cadence aarin-yara nipasẹ 5 spm ni gbogbo ọsẹ 2

Awọn itọkasi bọtini:

  • Karstoft K, et al. (2024).Appl Physiol Nutr Metab 49(1):1-15

Irinse ati Healthy ti ogbo

The Blue Zones Asopọ

Ungvari et al. (2023) ṣe atupale awọn aṣa irin-ajo ni Awọn agbegbe Buluu (awọn agbegbe pẹlu igbesi aye gigun ti o yatọ) ati awọn ọna molikula ti awọn ipa ipakokoro ti ogbo ti irin-ajo:

Iwọn Imudara ti o kere julọ fun Igba aye gigun

  • 30 iṣẹju / ọjọ, 5 ọjọ / ọsẹ= idinku eewu arun pataki
  • 150 iṣẹju / ọsẹ dede kikankikan(≥100 spm) = iwọn lilo to dara julọ
  • Le ti wa ni akojo ni bouts bi kukuru bi 10 iṣẹju

Anti-Ogbo Mechanisms ti Irinse

  • Ẹjẹ ọkan:Ṣe ilọsiwaju iṣẹ endothelial, dinku lile iṣan, dinku titẹ ẹjẹ
  • Metabolic:Ṣe ilọsiwaju ifamọ hisulini, ilọsiwaju ilana glukosi, mu idaabobo HDL pọ si
  • Ajesara:Dinku iredodo onibaje (↓CRP, ↓IL-6), mu iwo-kakiri ajesara pọ si
  • Cellular:Dinku aapọn oxidative, mu iṣẹ mitochondrial dara si, le fa gigun telomere
  • Ẹkọ-ara:Mu BDNF pọ, ṣe igbelaruge neurogenesis, mu sisan ẹjẹ ọpọlọ dara

Idinku Ewu Arun

Irin-ajo deede (30+ iṣẹju / ọjọ ni kikankikan dede) dinku eewu ti:

  • Arun inu ọkan ati ẹjẹ:30-40% idinku
  • Àtọgbẹ Iru 2:40-50% idinku
  • Gbogbo-okunfa iku:30-35% idinku
  • Iyawere:40% idinku
  • Ìsoríkọ́:25-30% idinku (fiwera si oogun)
  • Isubu ninu awọn agbalagba agbalagba:35% idinku pẹlu iwọntunwọnsi-lojutu irinse

Awọn itọkasi bọtini:

  • Ungvari Z, et al. (2023).GeroScience 45:3211–3239
  • Morris JN, Hardman AE (1997).idaraya Med 23(5):306-332 Alailẹgbẹ

Awọn Metiriki Iṣipopada Apple HealthKit

Awọn Aṣọ Onibara fun Iṣayẹwo Gait-Grade Clinical

Apple's iOS 14 (2020) ṣafihan awọn metiriki arinbo ilọsiwaju ti o gba data gait lainidi lati iPhone ati Apple Watch. Awọn metiriki wọnyi ti jẹ ifọwọsi ile-iwosan ati pe wọn lo ni bayi ninu iwadii iṣoogun.

Iyara Irinse

Oṣupa et al. (2023) ti fọwọsi iPhone Health app awọn wiwọn iyara gigun ni ilodi si awọn igbelewọn-ite iwadi:

  • Ibamu:r = 0.86-0.91 pẹlu awọn idanwo gigun-akoko goolu
  • Aṣiṣe Itumọ:0.05 m/s (itẹwọgba ni ile-iwosan)
  • Ibeere:iPhone 8+ ti a gbe sinu apo tabi apo lakoko irin-ajo adayeba

Iduroṣinṣin Irinse

Metiriki Irin-ajo Ohun-ini ti Apple (iṣafihan WWDC 2021) jẹ iwọn apapọ ti:

  • Iwontunwonsi ati iduroṣinṣin
  • Iyipada gigun gigun
  • Double support akoko
  • Aitasera iyara irinse

Irinse isọri Steadiness

  • O DARA:Ewu isubu kekere (<1% eewu isubu lododun)
  • Kekere:Ewu isubu dede (1-5% eewu isubu ọdọọdun)
  • Kekere pupọ:Ewu isubu giga (> 5% eewu isubu lododun) - nfa ifitonileti olumulo

Double Support Ogorun

Ogorun yiyipo gait nigbati ẹsẹ mejeeji wa lori ilẹ ni igbakanna:

  • Deede:20-30% ti gait ọmọ
  • Ti o ga (> 30-35%):Ṣe afihan igbẹkẹle iwọntunwọnsi ti o dinku tabi aisedeede
  • Ibẹrẹ ile-iwosan (> 35%):Ni pataki ni nkan ṣe pẹlu eewu isubu

Irinse Asymmetry

Iyatọ ogorun laarin osi ati awọn akoko igbesẹ ọtun:

  • Deede:<2-3% asymmetry
  • Ìwọ̀nba (> 3-5%):Le ṣe afihan aiṣedeede kekere tabi ailera
  • Pataki isẹgun (> 10%):Ṣe imọran ipalara, ipo iṣan-ara, tabi aiṣedeede gigun ẹsẹ

Irinse vs Nṣiṣẹ Biomechanics

Kini idi ti Irin-ajo kii ṣe Nṣiṣẹ lọra nikan

Irin-ajo ati ṣiṣiṣẹ jẹ ipilẹ ti o yatọ si awọn ilana mọto pẹlu biomechanics ọtọtọ, imuṣiṣẹ iṣan, ati awọn profaili ipalara (Mann et al., 1986; Fukuchi et al., 2019).

Isẹgun Lojo

  • Ipa kekere:Awọn ipa ipadasẹhin ilẹ ti o dinku ti irin-ajo jẹ ki o dara fun imularada ipalara, arthritis, ati osteoporosis
  • Olubasọrọ tẹsiwaju:Ipele atilẹyin ilọpo meji ṣe iwọntunwọnsi ati dinku eewu isubu vs ṣiṣiṣẹ
  • Iṣiṣẹ iṣan oriṣiriṣi:Irin-ajo n tẹnuba awọn glutes ati awọn okun ju ṣiṣe lọ
  • Kikan alagbero:Irin-ajo ngbanilaaye adaṣe gigun gigun ni awọn kikankikan sisun-sanra
