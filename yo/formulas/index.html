Irinse Metrics Fọọmu & Awọn aidọgba

Awọn ipilẹ mathematiki ti awọn atupale irin-ajo – awọn idogba ti a fọwọsi ni imọ-jinlẹ fun kikankikan, agbara, ati iṣẹ ṣiṣe

Oju-iwe yii ṣafihan awọn agbekalẹ ti imọ-jinlẹ ti a fọwọsi ti a lo ninu awọn atupale irin-ajo. Gbogbo awọn idogba jẹ itọkasi pẹlu awọn itọkasi iwadii ati awọn sakani deede ti a fọwọsi.

1. Cadence to METs Iyipada

Moore et al. (2021) Idogba Ipilẹṣẹ ti Cadence

Cadence to METs

METs = 0.0219 × Cadence (igbesẹ / iṣẹju) + 0.72

Yiye: ± 0.5 METs Moore CC, et al. (2021). Med Sci idaraya idaraya 53 (1): 165-173

Kini idi ti agbekalẹ yii ṣe pataki:Idogba yii jẹ23-35% diẹ sii deedeju awọn idogba orisun iyara ACSM ti aṣa fun irin-ajo. O ṣiṣẹ nitori cadence taara ṣe afihan igbohunsafẹfẹ gbigbe ati inawo agbara, lakoko ti iyara da lori gigun gigun gigun.

Awọn apẹẹrẹ:

Irin-ajo ni 100 spm:

METs = 0.0219 × 100 + 0.72 = 2.19 + 0.72 =2,91 METs

≈ 3 METs =Ibalẹ kikankikan dede ✓

Irin-ajo ni 110 spm:

METs = 0.0219 × 110 + 0.72 = 2.409 + 0.72 =3.13 METs

Ri to dede kikankikan

Irin-ajo ni 120 spm:

METs = 0.0219 × 120 + 0.72 = 2.628 + 0.72 =3,35 METs

Iwontunwonsi-kikankikan

Irin-ajo ni 130 spm:

METs = 0.0219 × 130 + 0.72 = 2.847 + 0.72 =3,57 METs

Ibalẹ agbara kikankikan(6 METs nipasẹ CADENCE-Agba wiwọn taara)

Akiyesi:Iwadi CADENCE-Awọn agbalagba ṣe iwọn taara pe 130 spm = 6 METs ni awọn ipo laabu iṣakoso. Idogba Moore jẹ apẹrẹ fun iwọn 80-130 spm ati pe o le ṣe aibikita ni awọn iwọn giga pupọ.

Data Ifọwọsi:

  • Apeere:76 agbalagba ori 21-40
  • Ọna:calorimetry aiṣe-taara (pawọn goolu)
  • Iye R²:0.87 (ibaṣepọ to dara julọ)
  • Itumọ aṣiṣe pipe:0,47 METs
  • Iwọn to wulo:80-130 igbesẹ / min

2. ACSM VO₂ Equations fun Irinse

ACSM Awọn iṣiro Metabolic

Irinse ipele (0% ite)

VO₂ (mL/kg/min) = 0.1 × Iyara (m/min) + 3.5

Iyara ni awọn mita fun iṣẹju kan (isodipupo km/h nipasẹ 16.67 tabi mph nipasẹ 26.82)

Irin-ajo pẹlu Ipele (ilọra / kọ)

VO₂ = 0.1 (Iyara) + 1.8 (Iyara) (Ipele) + 3.5

Iwọn ti a fihan bi eleemewa (fun apẹẹrẹ, 5% = 0.05)

Awọn apẹẹrẹ:

Irinse 5 km/h (83.3 m/min) lori ilẹ ipele:

VO₂ = 0.1 × 83.3 + 3.5 = 8.33 + 3.5 =11,83 milimita / kg / min

Yipada si METs: 11.83 / 3.5 =3,38 METs

Irin-ajo 5 km / h lori 5% idasi:

VO₂ = 0.1 (83.3) + 1.8 (83.3) (0.05) + 3.5

= 8.33 + 7.497 + 3.5 = 19,33 milimita / kg / min

= 19.33 / 3.5 = 5,52 METs

Ilọsiwaju pọ si kikankikan nipasẹ ~ 64%!

Awọn iyipada iyara:

  • km/h si m/min:isodipupo nipasẹ 16.67
  • mph si m/min:isodipupo nipasẹ 26.82
  • m/s si m/min:isodipupo nipasẹ 60

3. Lilo Lilo & Kalori Iná

Iṣiro Kalori deede

Awọn kalori fun iṣẹju kan

Cal/min = (METs × 3.5 × iwuwo ara kg) / 200

Lapapọ awọn kalori fun Ikoni

Lapapọ Awọn kalori = Cal/min × Iye akoko (iṣẹju)

Awọn apẹẹrẹ:

70 kg eniyan ti nrin 100 spm (3 METs) fun awọn iṣẹju 45:

Cal/min = (3 × 3.5 × 70) / 200 = 735/200 =3.675 cal / min

Lapapọ = 3.675 × 45 =165.4 awọn kalori

85 kg eniyan ti nrin 120 spm (5 METs) fun ọgbọn išẹju 30:

Cal/min = (5 × 3.5 × 85) / 200 = 1487.5/200 =7,44 cal / min

Lapapọ = 7.44 × 30 =223,2 awọn kalori

Kini idi ti agbekalẹ yii?

Idogba yii wa lati itumọ ti MET (Metabolic Equivalent of Task):

  • 1 MET = 3.5 milimita O₂/kg/min (oṣuwọn ijẹ-isimi isinmi)
  • 1 lita ti O₂ run ≈ 5 kcal sisun
  • Iyipada: (METs × 3.5 × kg × 5) / 1000 = (METs × 3.5 × kg) / 200

Isun Kalori Apapọ (Idaraya Nikan)

Awọn kalori apapọ (laisi isinmi)

Net Cal/min = [(METs - 1) × 3.5 × Iwọn Ara] / 200

Iyokuro 1 MET lati yọkuro awọn kalori ti o fẹ sun lonakona ni isinmi

70 kg, 3 METs, 45 min – Awọn kalori apapọ:

Àwọ̀n = [(3 - 1) × 3.5 × 70] / 200 × 45 = 2.45 × 45 =110.3 awọn kalori apapọ

vs 165.4 lapapọ awọn kalori (awọn kalori 55 yoo ti sun ni isinmi)

4. Atọka Symmetry Gait (GSI)

Didiwọn Asymmetry Osi-Ọtun

Gait Symmetry Atọka

GSI (%) = |Ọtun - Osi| / [0.5 × (Ọtun + Osi)] × 100

Le ṣee lo si gigun gigun, akoko igbesẹ, tabi akoko olubasọrọ

Itumọ:

  • <2-3%:Deede, mọnnnnkan alapọpo
  • 3-5%:Ìwọ̀nba asymmetry
  • 5-10%:Dede asymmetry, atẹle
  • >10%:Isẹgun pataki, ṣe ayẹwo ni ọjọgbọn

Awọn apẹẹrẹ:

Awọn akoko igbesẹ: Ọtun = 520 ms, Osi = 480 ms

GSI = |520 - 480| / [0.5 × (520 + 480)] × 100

= 40 / [0.5 × 1000] × 100 = 40 / 500 × 100 = 8% asymmetry

Iṣeduro iwọntunwọnsi - ronu lati mu ẹgbẹ alailagbara lagbara

Awọn gigun gigun: Ọtun = 1.42 m, Osi = 1.38 m

GSI = | 1.42 - 1.38| / [0.5 × (1.42 + 1.38)] × 100

= 0.04 / 1.4 × 100 = 2,86% asymmetry

Deede, ni ilera ibiti ✓

Akiyesi isẹgun:Apple HealthKit's Hiking Asymmetry nlo iṣiro diẹ ti o yatọ (iyatọ ipin ti o rọrun laarin awọn akoko igbesẹ) ṣugbọn awọn iloro itumọ jẹ iru.

5. Iwọn Ririn (Metiriki Ohun-ini Ti Awọn Itupalẹ Hike)

Irinse Idiyele

Dimegilio Ririn

WALK Dimegilio = Akoko (aaya) + Igbesẹ fun 100 mita

Isalẹ Dimegilio = dara ṣiṣe

Bi O Ṣe Nṣiṣẹ:

WALK Score daapọ akoko ati kika igbesẹ lati ṣe iwọn iṣẹ ṣiṣe irin-ajo. Arinkiri ti o bo 100m ni iṣẹju-aaya 75 pẹlu awọn igbesẹ 140 ni Iwọn WALK kan ti 215. Imudara boya iyara OR ṣiṣe ṣiṣe igbesẹ dinku Dimegilio.

Awọn apẹẹrẹ:

100m ni iṣẹju-aaya 80, awọn igbesẹ 120:

Dimegilio RIN = 80 + 120 =200

100m ni iṣẹju-aaya 70, awọn igbesẹ 110:

Dimegilio RIN = 70 + 110 =180

Iṣiṣẹ ti o dara julọ nipasẹ ilọsiwaju iyara + igbiyanju

100m ni iṣẹju-aaya 60, awọn igbesẹ 130 (irin-ajo ere-ije):

Dimegilio RIN = 60 + 130 =190

Awọn igbesẹ ti o yara ṣugbọn kukuru

Awọn sakani Aṣoju:

  • >250:O lọra / aisekokari mọnran, ṣee ṣe arinbo oran
  • 200-250:Àjọsọpọ hiker, apapọ ṣiṣe
  • 170-200:Irin-ajo amọdaju, ṣiṣe to dara
  • 150-170:Onitẹsiwaju ti o ni ilọsiwaju, ṣiṣe ti o dara julọ
  • <150:Gbajumo / ije irinse ipele

Ikẹkọ pẹlu Iwọn WALK:Tọpinpin Dimegilio rẹ lori ọna 100m kanna ni osẹ-ọsẹ. Awọn ilọsiwaju ṣe afihan isọdọkan neuromuscular imudara, agbara, ati eto-ọrọ irin-ajo.

6. Ipilẹ Gait Metiriki

Awọn iṣiro pataki

Iyara Irinse

Iyara (m/s) = Ijinna (m) / Akoko (awọn)

Cadence lati Total Igbesẹ

Cadence (spm) = Apapọ Igbesẹ / Aago (iṣẹju)

Gigun gigun

Gigun gigun (m) = Ijinna (m) / (Igbese / 2)

Pin awọn igbesẹ nipasẹ 2 nitori igbesẹ kan = igbesẹ meji

Igbesẹ Gigun

Igbesẹ Ipari (m) = Ijinna (m) / Awọn igbesẹ

Iyara lati Cadence & Gigun gigun

Iyara = Gigun gigun × (Cadence / 2) / 60

Tabi: Iyara (m/s) = Igbesẹ Gigun × Cadence / 60

Apeere Ṣiṣan Iṣẹ:

Gigun 1000m ni iṣẹju 12 pẹlu awọn igbesẹ 1320:

Iyara:1000m / 720s =1.39 m/s

Cadence:1320 igbesẹ / 12 min =110 spm

Gigun gigun:1000m / (1320/2) = 1000 / 660 =1.52 m

Gigun Igbesẹ:1000m / 1320 =0.76 m

7. Okan Rate Zone Awọn iṣiro

Ibile HR Zone Ọna

Iṣiro Oṣuwọn Ọkàn ti o pọju

Max HR = 220 - ori

Rọrun ṣugbọn ± 10-15 bpm iyatọ kọọkan

Omiiran: Fọọmu Tanaka (diẹ sii)

O pọju HR = 208 - (0.7 × Ọjọ ori)

Iṣiro Ibiti Agbegbe

Agbegbe = O pọju HR × (Isalẹ%, Oke%)

Apeere: omo ogoji odun

Ibile:Max HR = 220 - 40 =180 bpm

Tanaka:O pọju HR = 208 - (0.7 × 40) = 208 - 28 =180 bpm

Agbegbe 2 (60-70%):180 × 0.60 = 108 bpm si 180 × 0.70 = 126 bpm

Akiyesi:Lakoko ti awọn agbegbe HR wulo,Awọn agbegbe ti o da lori cadence jẹ deede diẹ sii ati ilowo fun irin-ajo(wo Itọsọna Awọn agbegbe Irin-ajo).

8. Iye owo ti Transport & Irinse Aje

Agbara iye owo ti Irinse

Iye owo gbigbe (C)

C = Lilo Agbara / (Ibi Ara × Ijinna)

Awọn iwọn: J/kg/m tabi mL O₂/kg/m

Ìtẹ́ Àpẹrẹ U-Eto-ọrọ irin-ajo tẹle ọna ti o ni apẹrẹ U. Iyara ti o dara julọ wa (ni deede 1.2-1.4 m/s tabi 4.3-5.0 km/h) nibiti iye owo gbigbe ti dinku. Irin-ajo lọra TABI yiyara ju eyi pọ si idiyele agbara fun irin-ajo ijinna kan.

Awọn Okunfa ti o ni ipa lori iye owo gbigbe:

  • Iyara:Ibasepo U-sókè (ti o dara julọ ni ayika 1.3 m/s)
  • Ilọsiwaju:Uphill significantly mu iye owo; ibosile posi eccentric iye owo
  • Iwọn ti ara:Awọn ẹni-kọọkan ti o wuwo ni idi ti o ga julọ ṣugbọn idiyele ibatan ti o jọra
  • Awọn mekaniki gigun:Gigun gigun to dara julọ dinku iye owo
  • Ilẹ̀:Uneven roboto mu iye owo vs dan pavement

9. Fifuye Ikẹkọ & Iwọn Wahala

Iwọn Wahala Irinse (WSS)

Agbegbe-orisun WSS

WSS = Σ (Awọn iṣẹju ni Ipin Agbegbe × agbegbe)

Agbegbe 1: ×1.0 | Agbegbe 2: ×2.0 | Agbegbe 3: ×3.0 | Agbegbe 4: ×4.0 | Agbegbe 5: ×5.0

Apeere: Irinse iṣẹju 60

10 min Agbegbe 1 × 1 = 10 ojuami

40 min Agbegbe 2 × 2 = 80 ojuami

10 min Agbegbe 3 × 3 = 30 ojuami

Lapapọ WSS = 120

Osẹ Ikẹkọ Fifuye

Osẹ fifuye

Ikojọpọ Ọsẹ = Σ WSS Ojoojumọ (ọjọ meje)

Onitẹsiwaju apọju

Ọsẹ to nbọ = Ọsẹ ti o wa lọwọlọwọ × 1.05-1.10

Mu iwọn 5-10% pọ si ni ọsẹ kan

Ọsẹ imularada

Ọsẹ Ìgbàpadà = Lọwọlọwọ × 0.50-0.70

Ni gbogbo ọsẹ 3-4, dinku si 50-70%

Awọn ẹru Ọsẹ Aṣoju:

  • Arinkiri ilera alakọbẹrẹ:200-400 WSS / ọsẹ
  • Arinrin adaṣe deede:400-700 WSS / ọsẹ
  • Arinkiri amọdaju to ṣe pataki:700-1000 WSS / ọsẹ
  • Arìnrìn àjò tí ó lè díje:1000-1500+ WSS / ọsẹ

10. Awọn idogba asọtẹlẹ

6-Minute Hike igbeyewo (6MWT) Ijinna Asọtẹlẹ

Ijinna 6MWT ti asọtẹlẹ (Enright & Sherrill)

Awọn ọkunrin:(7.57 × Giga cm) - (5.02 × Ọjọ ori) - (1.76 × iwuwo kg) - 309

Awọn obinrin:(2.11 × Giga cm) - (5.78 × Ọjọ ori) - (2.29 × iwuwo kg) + 667

Ṣe asọtẹlẹ ijinna ni awọn mita fun awọn agbalagba ti o ni ilera

Apeere: Okunrin 40 odun, 175 cm, 75 kg

6MWT = (7.57 × 175) - (5.02 × 40) - (1.76 × 75) - 309

= 1324.75 - 200.8 - 132 - 309 = 682.95 mita

Agbara iṣẹ ṣiṣe to dara fun ọjọ ori

Lilo isẹgun:6MWT ni a lo lati ṣe ayẹwo agbara idaraya iṣẹ ni awọn alaisan inu ọkan ati ẹjẹ, iṣaju iṣaju / lẹhin-abẹ-abẹ, ati amọdaju gbogbogbo ni awọn agbalagba agbalagba.
