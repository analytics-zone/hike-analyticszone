Bibliography na Hiking Analytics

Cikakkun nassoshi na kimiyya da binciken bincike masu goyan bayan nazarin tafiye-tafiye, nazarin gait, da ma'aunin lafiya

Wannan littafin littafin yana ba da cikakkiyar shaidar kimiyya da ke tallafawa awo, dabaru, da shawarwarin da aka yi amfani da su a cikin Hike Analytics. Duk nassoshi sun haɗa da hanyoyin haɗin kai kai tsaye zuwa wallafe-wallafen da aka duba.

1. Matakai, Ƙarfi, da Lafiya

Ina K, et al. (2023)

"Haɗin Tsarin Matakan Yau da kullun tare da Mutuwa a cikin Manyan Amurka"

JAMA Network Bude2023; 6 (3): e235174

Nazarin manya 4,840 na Amurka yana nuna cewa matakai 8,000-9,000/rana a cikin manya suna rage mace-mace. Fa'idodin plateau fiye da wannan kewayon, yana ba da shawarar raguwar dawowa a mafi girman matakan ƙididdiga.

Duba Labari →

Lee I-M, et al. (2019)

"Ƙungiyar Ƙarfin Mataki da Ƙarfi Tare da Duk-Dalilin Mutuwar Mata a cikin Manya"

JAMA Maganin Ciki 2019;179(8):1105-1112

Nazarin tsofaffin mata 16,741 (yana nufin shekaru 72) yana nuna raguwar mace-mace tare da matakan ≥4,400 / rana, tare da fa'idodin da ke bayyana kusan matakai 7,500 a rana. Tabbatar da shaida cewa "ƙarin ba koyaushe mafi kyau ba ne."

Cikakken meta-bincike yana ba da alaƙar amsa kashi tsakanin matakan yau da kullun da sakamakon lafiya a tsakanin al'ummomi daban-daban.

2. Cadence da tsanani

Tudor-Locke C, et al. (2019) - Nazarin CADENCE- Manya

"Tsarin tafiya (matakai / min) da ƙarfi a cikin shekarun 21-40: CADENCE-manyan"

Jarida ta kasa da kasa na Abincin Abinci da Ayyukan Jiki 2019;16:8

Nazarin ƙasa yana kafa matakai 100/min a matsayin kofa don matsakaicin ƙarfi (3 METs)tare da 86% hankali da 89.6% takamaiman a cikin mahalarta 76 masu shekaru 21-40. Wannan binciken ya samar da tushe don sa ido kan ƙarfin aiki a cikin tafiye-tafiye.

Duba Labari →

Tudor-Locke C, et al. (2020)

"Tsarin tafiya (matakai / min) da ƙarfi a cikin tsofaffi masu shekaru 41 zuwa 60: nazarin CADENCE- manya"

Jarida ta kasa da kasa na Abincin Abinci da Ayyukan Jiki 2020;17:137

An tabbatar da iyakar 100 spm don matsakaicin matsakaici a cikin manya masu shekaru (41-60). An kafa 130 spm a matsayin ƙofa don ƙarfin ƙarfi (6 METs).

Duba Labari →

Aguiar EJ, et al. (2021)

"Cadence (matakai / min) da ƙarfin dangi a cikin 21 zuwa 60 masu shekaru: nazarin CADENCE-adult"

Jarida ta kasa da kasa na Abincin Abinci da Ayyukan Jiki 2021;18:27

Meta-binciken da ke tabbatar da ƙofofin ƙididdiga sun kasance masu karko a cikin shekaru 21-85, suna goyan bayan aikace-aikacen duniya mai ƙarfi na sa ido mai ƙarfi.

Duba Labari →

Moore CC, et al. (2021)

"Haɓaka Ma'auni na tushen Cadence don Hiking"

Magunguna & Kimiyya a Wasanni & Motsa jiki 2021;53(1):165-173

Ƙirƙirar ma'auni mai sauƙi:METs = 0.0219 × adadin + 0.72. Wannan samfurin ya nuna 23-35% mafi girman daidaito fiye da daidaitattun daidaiton ACSM, tare da madaidaicin ~0.5 METs a saurin tafiya na yau da kullun.

Jarida ta kasa da kasa na Abincin Abinci da Ayyukan Jiki 2022;19:1

Shaida ta farko don bincike mai ƙarfi-ƙarfin a cikin ƙungiyoyin shekaru, yana ba da cikakkiyar tsari don fassarar.

3. Gudun Gait, Ragewa, da Faɗuwa

"Gudun Tafiya da Rayuwa a cikin Manya"
