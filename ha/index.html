Cadence Kan Yawan Zuciya

Me ya sa yake da mahimmanci:Yawan zuciya ya bambanta da zafi, damuwa, maganin kafeyin, rashin lafiya. Cadence da abin dogara, mai amfani, kuma tabbatacce. Nazarin CADENCE-Alds ya nuna 100 spm = matsakaicin matsakaici tare da 86% hankali-mafi daidai fiye da kimantawar HR.

Kololuwa-30 Binciken Cadence

Ma'aunin nasara:Mafi kyawun ku na mintuna 30 a jere na yawo kowace rana daban yayi hasashen lafiyar zuciya da kuma mace-mace (masu halartar bankin Biobank 78,500). Muna bin wannan kullun-babu wani hiking app yayi.

Rigakafin Rauni tare da ACWR

Kimiyyar wasanni da aka daidaita don yawo:Saka idanu Mai Mutuwar ku: Matsayin Nauyin Aiki na yau da kullun don hanawa raunin da ya wuce kima. Bincike ya nuna ACWR> 1.50 = 2-4 × hadarin rauni. Muna faɗakar da ku kafin hatsaniya ta afku.

Haɓaka Tattalin Arziki na Hiking

Ingancin makamashi:Bibiyar Kudin Sufuri da ingancin tafiya (EF, Rabo Tsaye). Inganta tattalin arziki ta hanyar 10-15% ta hanyar horon Zone 2, inganta haɓakawa, da ƙarfin aiki - tare da takamaiman jagora.

Gait Symmetry & Faɗuwar Hadarin

Ma'auni na asibiti:Yi lissafin Gait Symmetry Index (GSI) don gano asymmetry. Biyu goyon baya> 35% da saurin tafiya <0.8m/s suna nuna haɗarin faɗuwar faɗuwa-mafi mahimmanci ga tsofaffi da gyarawa.

Fiye da 50+ Abubuwan Da Aka Duba Tsara

Jagoran tushen shaida:Kowace shawarwari tana haɗi zuwabincike da aka buga. Daga Tudor-Locke's cadence ƙofa zuwa Alamar saurin tafiya ta Studenski - nuna gaskiya a cikin kimiyya.

Fitness Hikers

Manya (65+)

Saka idanusaurin tafiya a matsayin alama mai mahimmanci(tsayawa> 1.0 m/s). Bibiyar tallafi sau biyu % (<35%=Kyakkyawan natsuwa), asymmetry, da faɗuwar alamun haɗari. Ganewar farko na raguwar motsi.

Marasa lafiya Gyaran jiki

Haƙiƙa bibiyar haɓakar gait tare da dabarar GSI (asymmetry <5%=mai kyau), tsayin tsayin tsayin daka, da ci gaban gudun tafiya. Yi daftarin sakamako ga masu ba da lafiya.

Rage nauyi & Lafiya

Inganta ƙona kalori tare da horon yanki. Yi lissafin kashe kuɗin makamashi daidai da ƙarancin Moore →METs daidaito. Gina halayen motsa jiki masu ɗorewa tare da ɗaukar nauyi a hankali.

Me yasa Hike Analytics ya bambanta da sauran ƙa'idodin yawo?

Mu ne kawai ƙa'idodin yawo da aka gina akan bincike-bincike na tsara. Kowane awo - Peak-30 cadence, ACWR, Farashin Transport - ya fito daga binciken da aka buga. Mun buga 50+ takardun kimiyya da kuma bayyana shaida a bayan kowane shawarwarin. Ba kidayar mataki ba ce; biomechanics ne da ilimin halittar motsa jiki da ake amfani da shi don yin yawo.

Menene Peak-30 cadence kuma me yasa yake da mahimmanci?

Peak-30 cadence shine matsakaicin matakan ku a cikin minti ɗaya yayin mafi kyawun mintuna 30 a jere na yawo kowace rana. Wani bincike mai zurfi na mutane 78,500 (Del Pozo-Cruz, JAMA 2022) ya nuna kansa yana tsinkayar mace-mace. haɗari-ko da bayan sarrafawa don jimlar matakan yau da kullun. Muna bin wannan ta atomatik, wani abu da babu wani app da yake yi.

Ta yaya ACWR ke hana raunuka?

Matsakaicin: Rage yawan Aiki na yau da kullun yana kwatanta horonku na baya-bayan nan (kwanaki 7 na ƙarshe) zuwa matsakaicin tsawon lokacinku (28 kwanaki). Bincike ya nuna ACWR> 1.50 yana ƙara haɗarin rauni sau 2-4. Muna lissafin wannan kullun kuma muna yi muku gargaɗi kafin masu haɗari masu haɗari, yana taimaka muku ci gaba lafiya tare da haɓaka 5-10% na mako-mako.

Me yasa yankunan cadence maimakon yankunan bugun zuciya?

Cadence ya fi dogara da aiki. Nazarin CADENCE-Alds ya tabbatar da 100 spm = matsakaicin matsakaici (3 METs) tare da 86% hankali da 90% takamaiman. Yawan zuciya ya bambanta da zafi, damuwa, rashin lafiya, maganin kafeyin-cadence ba. Bugu da kari, ba kwa buƙatar madaurin ƙirji ko agogo; kawai kirga matakai.

Shin Hike Analytics na iya taimakawa tare da rigakafin faɗuwa?

Ee. Muna bin alamun haɗarin faɗuwar asibiti: saurin tafiya <0.8m/s, tallafi biyu>35%, asymmetry (GSI)> 10%, da kwanciyar hankali. Waɗannan ƙofofin tushen shaida ne daga binciken geriatric. Ganewa da wuri yana ba da damar shiga tsakani kafin faɗuwa ya faru-mahimmanci ga manya 65+.

Ta yaya Hike Analytics ke kare sirrina?

Duk bayanan suna tsayawa akan lokacin iPhone-lokaci. Babu daidaitawar gajimare, babu asusu, babu sabar da ke karɓar bayanan lafiyar ku. Mu aiwatar da komai a cikin gida ta amfani da algorithms akan na'urar. Ma'auni na tafiya, wuri, bugun zuciya - gaba ɗaya na sirri. Muna bin GDPR da falsafar HIPAA duk da cewa ba a buƙatar mu bisa doka (tun da ba mu taɓa gani ba). data ka).

Ina bukatan kayan aiki na musamman?

A'a. Hike Analytics yana aiki tare da iPhone ko Apple Watch. Don ma'auni na asali (cadence, nisa, matakai), kawai naka waya. Don bayanan bugun zuciya da ƙarin ingantattun ma'auni, Apple Watch yana taimakawa amma ba a buƙata ba. Muna hadewa tare da Apple Health, don haka kowace na'ura mai jituwa tana aiki.

Shin Hike Analytics ya dace don gyarawa?

Lallai. Ana amfani da mu ta marasa lafiya na rehab suna bin diddigin murmurewa da gaske. Tsarin Gait Symmetry Index (GSI). yana ƙididdige bambance-bambancen hagu-dama (<3% al'ada,> 10% mahimmancin asibiti). Saka idanu tsawon farfadowa, Inganta saurin tafiya, da rage asymmetry. Raba bayanan haƙiƙa tare da likitan ku na jiki ko likita.

Menene tushen kimiyya don dabaru 11?

Kowannedabaraya haɗa da ingantattun bayanai da ƙididdiga na bincike na asali. Misali: Moore's cadence→METs equation (R²=0.87, ±0.5 METs daidaito, manya 76) shine 23-35% mafi daidai fiye da tsofaffi ACSM equations. Muna nuna kimiyya, ba kawai lissafin akwatin akwatin ba.
